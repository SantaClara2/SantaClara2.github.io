Welcome to Activity Days in 2024! - If you are turning 8 this year (CTR 7) or are born in the years 2013-2016, you are invited! We meet the 3rd Wednesday of every month from 7:00-8:00pm, usually in the primary room.
Next activity is Wednesday, January 17th. Contact Allison Carman with any questions: 503-816-6005. See you there!


Relief Society Conference – The Santa Clara Stake Relief Society Conference will be held at the Danebo building on Saturday, January 27th, from 9:30 am -11:00 am. We will have a service project and a series of workshops designed to help us as we strive to connect with Christ. Lunch will be served at 11 am. Bring a friend!


Ward Temple Recommend Interviews are held the 2nd Tuesday of every month at the Danebo building. 6:00 – 7:00 pm. Please contact the Ward Executive Secretary, Clint Chandler, at 458-201-9013 to make an appointment.
